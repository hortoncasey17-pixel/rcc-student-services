Canvas Login Guide

Simple steps to access your RCC online courses.

info_outline

What is Canvas?

Canvas is RCC's online learning system. It is where you will find your course materials, assignments, grades, and messages from your instructor.

RCC is transitioning to Canvas this summer. Canvas replaces Moodle as our online learning platform.

looks_onePhase 1: Get Into Canvas
1
Get Your Login Credentials

Before you can log in you need your RCC username and password. Your username is usually your student email address. If you do not have your login information contact the IT Help Desk right away.

phoneCall IT Help Desk — (910) 272-3566
2
Go to the Canvas Website

Open any web browser. Go to robeson.instructure.com or visit robeson.edu and look for the Canvas link.

open_in_newOpen Canvas Website
3
Log In for the First Time

Enter your RCC username and password, then click Log In. If it is your first time logging in you may be asked to set up your account.

looks_twoPhase 2: Find Your Class
4
Find Your Dashboard

After logging in you will see your Dashboard. Your courses appear as cards or tiles. If you do not see your course it may not be published yet — contact your advisor.

5
Open Your Course

Click on the course card. In the left menu you will find: Home, Modules, Syllabus, Grades, and Announcements.

looks_3Phase 3: Do Your Work
6
Find and Submit Assignments

Click Assignments or Modules, click the assignment name, read the instructions, click Submit Assignment, upload your file or type your answer, then click Submit again.

7
Check Your Grades

Click Grades in the left menu to see all your scores. If you have a question about a grade, message your instructor through Canvas.

8
Message Your Instructor

Click the Inbox icon, click Compose, search for your instructor's name, type your message and click Send. Always include your full name and course name.

9
Turn On Notifications

Click your profile picture, click Notifications, and turn on Email or Text notifications for Announcements and Grades.

help_outline

Need Help?

Canvas also has 24/7 student support. Click the Help button inside Canvas for live chat or phone support at any time.
